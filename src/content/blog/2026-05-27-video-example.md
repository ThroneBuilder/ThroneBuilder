This is a sample article demonstrating the YouTube embed feature. The video above plays directly on ThroneBuilder.com using a privacy-enhanced player that loads no tracking cookies until you press play.

## How Videos Work Here

Videos from the ThroneBuilder YouTube channel are linked in article frontmatter. The site extracts the video ID and renders a responsive, lazy-loaded embed. If the URL is absent or unrecognized, the article renders normally — the written content is always the primary deliverable.

Replace the `youtubeUrl` in this article's frontmatter with any public ThroneBuilder YouTube video to test with real content.
